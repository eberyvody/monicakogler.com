Flier

I'm a cofounder and the Chief Creative Officer of Flier, an open-source file selector widget. It's designed to simplify the life of app developers and to address growing data fragmentation. It presents multiple cloud storage services in a unified interface.

LoopKit

I'm a cofounder the the Chief Creative Officer of LoopKit. LoopKit allows you to share short audio loops with your friends! You can add as many layers as you like to a loop. This app is still in development.

Zapd

I did production work for Zapd, including cross-platform adjustments, photography research, and fabrication of default images and templates. Zapd lets you create instant themed websites from your phone. It's an Apple Staff Pick, App of the Week, and Top Ten App in 20 countries.

Butterfly Radio

I was redesigned the UI and UX for Butterfly Radio, an app that allows you to easily run an internet radio station directly from your phone. My audits are still in development.
